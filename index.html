Elegant Tiered Cakes

If you’re having a stylish event, especially a wedding, it demands to have a centerpiece – and let’s face it, nobody’s having their picture taken with the pesto. Our cakes are delicious, but just as important, they are something you’ll have photos and fond memories of for years to come.

People often ask us, “Isn’t it a shame to eat something so beautiful?” Our answer is simple: No. What would be a shame is if you didn’t eat it. While a cake’s material life may be short, it is remembered far longer and more fondly than most sculptures, paintings or more permanent forms of art could ever hope to be. We think you should make the memory count with a cake that tastes as good as it looks.

If you’re interested in potentially ordering a wedding cake, we recommend setting up a wedding cake tasting, where we can sit down with you, learn about your event, and design a custom cake just for you.

Elegant Tiered Cakes, such as wedding cakes, have an average price of $8 – $16 per serving. The minimum order for any custom cake is $250.

The exact price of a cake is dependent on its size and detail. Our general pricing guideline is $6 per serving plus $75 per hour of decorating, however we often present the pricing in a per-serving manner for ease of budgeting and comparison.

Because our pricing is linked to our time, if you let us know what your budget and serving requirements are, we can come up with a design to meet your goals. Many of our designs can be varied to be more detailed and awesome, or less so, based on your individual requirements.

Cupcakes, Cookies, and Dessert Bars are also available!
